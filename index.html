TOC - The Ocean Cleaner

Clean the oceans with your harpoon!

Important Information before Your Start:

The Challenge: Free the oceans from as much plastic as possible!
You can catch a maximum of 10 ocean dwellers per dive.
Never collect more sea dwellers than plastic, otherwise you've lost!

Oxygen level: 100%.
Dive until your oxygen storage needs a recharge.
Harpoon shots: 8 reloaded.

Use arrow keys: to dive up, dive down, dive forward, dive backward.
Use spacebar: to shoot with your harpoon.

Never give up - good luck!
